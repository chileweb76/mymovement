# GI Tracker

## Application Definition Statement

Essentially the idea is for a poop tracker. In other words an app that allows you to look at foods, medications, and situations that could cause gut issues so that they may be avoided in the future. 

## Target Market
This is a wide open market. Potential users could be as simple as a low income, unemployed mother who suffers from gut issues all the way to a CEO of a major company who is has a high level income. 

Doctors could suggest to their patients the app. 

## User Profile

48 year old woman with minimal income suffers from gut issues on a regular basis. She wants to pinpoint where the issues may be coming from. Tracking her diet and what foods she eats and the gut results. Taking in consideration medications and other factors like stress. 

## Use Cases

User types in what they ate whether it is a recipe that they made or going from a bar code on a product. Ability to add a picture would be helpful in visualizing the food and quick reference. The end results are entered in another field. 


## Problem Statement

They have unexplainable gut issues ranging from gut pain to constant diarrhea. Tests don't point to a specific illness. They have no way of pinpoint problem areas that they can address with their doctor.

## Pain Points

Gut issues which can include diagnosed problems such as irritable bowel syndrom or Crohn's disease. These issues can impact one's life comfort. 

## Solution Statement

The project would give them a picture of what to avoid to lessen or eliminated their gut issues. It would give doctors a snapshot to their patients diet and other contributing factors. 

There are several diet apps on the market but they focus mainly on weight loss and other dietary goals without addressing gut issues and the goal of solving them or at least minimizing them. 

## Competition

Competing dietary apps focus more on weight loss and allergy issues.  They don't address gut issues and what could be causing them. 

## Key Features

Product ingredients input or retrieval connected to resulting issues. The ability to input medications. The ability to address stessors. Pictures that allow quick looks. 

## Integrations

This API allows the use of barcodes or general food searching for information purposes. The app would utilize these to give the user an idea of what they ate.
